Soup Dish

Congee with carrots and chicken

Rice
Carrots
Chicken Stock
Shredded Chicken

Put the rice in a pot with the chicken stock and bring to boil. Give the rice a good stir
scraping the bottom of the pan to prevent sticking
Put small, chopped carrots into the pot
Constantly stirring until the rice bursted open
Season with light salt and fish sauce

Green Lettuce Soup

Green Lettuce
Ground Pork

Season ground pork with fish sauce, MSG, put into the pot and fries until it is nearly cooked
Put 1 bowl of water into the pot and wait until boil
Put the green lettuce in
Add a touch of fish sauce and MSG

Meat Dish

Beef Saute with Green Beans

Beef
Green Beans
1tsp Fish Sauce, 1tsp Oyster Sauce, 4tsp Dark Soy Sauce
onions, garlic

Season beef with a oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, chopped garlic, salts and pepper
Put beans on pan, with cutted onions and saute it with a touch of fish sauce
After beans are cooked throughly, put the beef in until it is cook

Honey Chicken Wings on Pan

Chicken Wings
1/4 cup of Honey, red chili pepper
1tsp of vinegar, 1/2 cup of dark soy garlic
garlic(lot of garlic), Green onions

Seasoned chicken with honey, vineger, dark soy garlic, garlic(a lot), and red chili pepper
Wait for 15 to 30 minutes
Put on pan and fried until the skins on the chicken is a bit crunchy
cut of green onions and put it on top for decoration

Pan Fried Salmon

Salmon
butter
garlic

Season salmon with salt(a lot of salt on the skin)
Melt the butter and turn on high heat, put the salmon with the skin head first on pan
Check until the skin is somewhat crunchy, lower the heat and start to cook the salmon
Put some chopped garlic in and wait until the Salmon is a bit before "welldone" (else it become dry)
